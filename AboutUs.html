Our Story

Athul Asokan

Sneha Biju

TechComm Insights was born out of a shared passion for technology and communication. Both Sneha Biju and Athul Asokan, the co-founders of this platform, were inspired by their experiences working in the tech industry. They realized the crucial role that effective communication plays in the success of any technological endeavor.

Sneha, with his background as a senior technical writer, witnessed firsthand the challenges of conveying complex technical concepts to diverse audiences. Meanwhile, Athul, a seasoned UX designer, recognized the importance of user-centric communication in creating seamless digital experiences.

Driven by their mutual belief in the power of clear and concise communication, Sneha and Athul embarked on a mission to create TechComm Insights. Their goal was to establish a platform where professionals, enthusiasts, and aspiring individuals could access valuable resources, insights, and best practices in the field of technical communication.

TechComm Insights serves as a hub for sharing knowledge, fostering collaboration, and staying updated on the latest trends in technology communication. Sneha and Athul are dedicated to empowering others to excel in their respective roles and contribute to the advancement of the tech industry through effective communication.
